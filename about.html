Learn About Our History

Our Mission

Founded with heart and vision, our care home has grown into a trusted space for seniors seeking long or short-term assistance. From the very beginning, the idea was simple: create a warm, nurturing place where residents are not just cared for, but truly feel at home.

Over the years, our team has continuously evolved to meet modern care standards while maintaining the spirit of compassion and dignity that defines us. Today, we are proud to offer both medical support and meaningful connection in an atmosphere that feels like family.

Our Facilities

We currently offer 42 beds, including a mix of single and shared rooms designed with comfort and privacy in mind. Our spacious sitting room encourages community and relaxation, while our bright, open-plan kitchen delivers daily home-cooked meals tailored to each resident’s needs. Special events, seasonal activities, and visiting performances enrich the social calendar and ensure every day feels special.

What We Offer

  • Long and Short-Term Care
  • Post-Operative / Surgical Recovery
  • Non-Acute Medical Support
  • Convalescent and Respite Services
  • Palliative and End-of-Life Care
